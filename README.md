### 1. Data Engineering (Building the Warehouse)
The core of this project involves consolidating sales data into a unified structure.

* **Data Sources:**
    * **ERP System:** Source 1 (CSV Format)
    * **CRM System:** Source 2 (CSV Format)
* **Data Quality:** Implementation of cleaning procedures to resolve inconsistencies and quality issues before analysis.
* **Integration:** Merging ERP and CRM data into a single, user-friendly data model.
* **Scope:** Focused on the **latest dataset** (Snapshot model); Historical data versioning (SCD) is out of scope for this iteration.
* **Documentation:** Comprehensive documentation of the schema for stakeholders and analytics teams.

### 2. Business Intelligence (Analytics & Reporting)
Once the data is modeled, I develop SQL queries to extract insights.

* **üìä Customer Behavior:** Analyzing purchasing patterns and customer segmentation.
* **üìà Product Performance:** Tracking sales volume, revenue, and product trends.
